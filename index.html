My dearest love,

As Valentine's Day approaches, I find myself lost for words. I am overwhelmed by the depth of my feelings for you and I am at a loss for how to express them. I know that on this special day, people all over the world are expressing their love to their loved ones, and I want to do the same.

I know that I am not by your side to celebrate this day with you, but please know that my love for you transcends time and distance. I am working tirelessly towards my life goals, so that one day, I can be there for you and make you the happiest person in the world.

I know that you may not be ready to listen to my words right now, but deep in your heart, I am confident that you will soon feel the love that I have for you. I am patient, and I am willing to wait for you until the day that you are ready to accept my love.

Until then, please know that you are always on my mind and in my heart. I hope and pray that we will be together soon, so that we can share our love on Valentine's Day and every day like it's Valentine's Day. Actually, the day I saw you is my special day.

Yours always
